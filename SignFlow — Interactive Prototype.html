About this prototype
SignFlow is a purpose-built SaaS platform to manage real estate sign installation, removal, service calls, and deliveries for Sign Up Sign Services Inc. This interactive prototype covers all major screens across the admin portal, agent portal, and installer mobile app. All screens are fully clickable and interactive.
For Simform: This prototype represents the agreed-upon design specification. Please treat each screen as the primary UI/UX reference. The Scope of Work document is linked below and covers all functional requirements, data architecture decisions, and technical specifications.
